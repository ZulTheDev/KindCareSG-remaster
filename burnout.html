Are you Lazy? Or
Burnout?

Burnout is a state of tiredness by your emotional well-being that will soon trigger your physical well-being.
Did you know that 75% of Singaporeans suffer from burnout? Imagine if we calculate that to the amount of the entire global.
That's about a quarter of our society who suffer from it.

What are the types of burnout?

Physical Burnout Emotional Burnout Cognitive Burnout Behavioral Burnout

Physical Burnout

Exhaustion, fatigue, and even physical symptoms like headaches. Take a break, prioritize self-care, and seek support.

Emotional Burnout

Feeling emotionally drained? It's okay to express your feelings. Connect with others and practice mindfulness to manage emotional burnout.

Cognitive Burnout

Struggling with focus and mental exhaustion? Break tasks into smaller steps, take short breaks, and engage in activities that relax your mind.

Behavioral Burnout

Experiencing changes in behavior? It's a sign of burnout. Prioritize healthy habits, communicate your needs, and seek professional support if necessary.
